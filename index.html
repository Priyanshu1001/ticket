Welcome to our ticket booking website

Guidelines


1. Be careful while you book ticket through any online portal.
2. Never share your personal details to any person in order to book the ticket.
3. After making a transaction please do wait unless the confirmation/rejection message is received
4. It may take 10-20 minutes
5. In case you are cancelling a ticket, then the appropriate amount will be refunded within 24-48 hours.

Rules


1. Always maintain a copy of your ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc...,)
2. Possession of any kind of illegal materials(drugs, alcohols, flammabkes, etc...,) can be sentenced with prison of 2-3 years and a fine of Rs. 10000-90000.
3. Creating any kind of chaos while travelling can be a crimainal offence and may be sentenced with 2 years prison and a fine of 5000 -50000.
4. Occupying other passenger's seat is also a offence and you may be asked to leave the train in next station.
